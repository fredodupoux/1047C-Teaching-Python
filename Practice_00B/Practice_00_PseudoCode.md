# OIP

### OUTPUT
- Item Original Cost
- Discount Amount
- Final Cost

### INPUT
- Item Original Cost
- Discount Rate

### PROCESS
1. Calculate Discount Amount: \( \text{Original Cost} \times \text{Discount Rate} \)
2. Calculate Final Cost: \( \text{Original Cost} - \text{Discount Amount} \)
